Business Domain Chosen: Transportation & Logistics

For this assignment, the chosen business domain is Transportation and Logistics, specifically focusing on ride demand and operational efficiency analysis.
This sector plays a crucial role in modern urban mobility, where companies like Uber continuously optimize routes, driver allocation, and customer experience. The logistics industry depends heavily on data to improve delivery speed, minimize idle times, and reduce costs. Analyzing such datasets enables companies to understand real-time demand fluctuations, customer behavior, and route performance, which are critical for achieving business efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Data Source

The dataset used for this analysis is the Uber Pickups Dataset, available publicly on Kaggle.

Dataset Link:
Uber Pickups in New York City (Kaggle)

This dataset contains records of Uber rides and pickup patterns across New York City during 2014, originally published by FiveThirtyEight for transportation analysis research.

Business Questions to Answer

The objective of this analysis is to uncover operational insights that can enhance decision-making for a logistics or ride-sharing company.
Key questions explored include:

When does peak demand occur?
Identify the hours and days with the highest ride activity to optimize driver scheduling.

Which locations have the highest pickup frequency?
Helps in planning regional resource allocation and identifying underserved zones.

How do weekdays and weekends differ in ride patterns?
Supports targeted deployment for corporate vs. leisure routes.

What is the relationship between trip distance and duration?
Reveals route efficiency and potential optimization areas.

What are the most common purposes of rides?
Aids in developing customer segmentation and business-oriented service models.
